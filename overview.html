Tapio Overview

Tapio is a complete engagement system which allows for digital social engagement and interaction through real-world objects.

Tapio uses advanced technologies, including near field communication (NFC) tags, to enable a wide variety of social and business interactions through your mobile device.

Built for Creators

Creators, including artists, architects, businesses, and institutions can embed these tags within objects, spaces, surfaces, and products to create a custom ‘digital fingerprint’ that is as unique as an individual.

Built for Businesses

Galleries and museums can embed Tapio tags and allow their visitors to digital ‘collect’ works of art, learn about artists, and build their own collection of works they love.

Built for Interaction

Restaurants and stores can use Tapio tags to build dynamic environments which are engaging, powerful and fun.

Once placed, Tapio creates an immediate and meaningful connection between visitors and customers with creators and organizations.

How it Works

Explore Tapio in a Few Simple Steps

Tapio works by harnessing the NFC capabilities in most phones. The majority of leading Android phones come embedded with NFC natively, while iPhones are now emerging with this technology. For phones which are not enabled, leverage our innovative QR-style codes and scan Tapio tags to unlock the same features.

Tapio tags are completely unique, meaning that you can track, and differentiate, between different designed experiences. Your tags are uniquely yours, and link your customers directly to your business.

Tapio tags are linked with Tapio-enabled mobile applications which allow you to get instant notifications whenever your tags are tapped. Track analytics on how interactions occur, connect with your clients and customers immediately, and share special media, information and rewards to your loyal visitors.

Leverage our Tapio Portal to track the entire history of interaction with your brand, your space, and see how your tags and customers are interacting.

1. Create your Tapio Subscription.

Your Tapio Subscription is your gateway to designing your interactions with your customers. 

2. Order your Tags

Order as many, or as few tags, as you like. Order single Tapio tags or tags in the thousands. They will be programmed and linked directly to your subscription.  They are unique.  

3. Use the Portal

Use the Tapio Portal and design the experience you want to trigger through each tag.  Select from our features or build your own custom experience.

4. Unleash your Tags

Tags can be placed almost anywhere.  On your creations, your products, your spaces, or your materials.  It is completely up to you!  Tag responsibly :)

5. Track Tags

Track your business intelligence through the Tapio Portal or use the Tapio App and to receive instant notifications or engage through your smartphone.
